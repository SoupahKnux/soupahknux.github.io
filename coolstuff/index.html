This is a list of (maybe) relatively not well-known things that I feel like are worth checking out if you are also bored as hell. Might add more things in the future!

MakeSweet

Pretty sure you know about those gifs where the heart locket opens to reveal an image? Yes, that's the website where people make this stuff.

Bad Translator

"An authentic "bad translator" so that you can create terrible Engrish-style translations for your shop signs or adverts or whatever."

MILK Kommunikations Ko-Op

Dan Bornstein bought the milk.com domain in 1994 and refuses to sell out (for less than $10M, at least). And now that's his personal website. Funny things.

xkcd

Doubles as home for the webcomic "xkcd" and as Randall Munroe's personal website. You likely saw his comics on the Internet one or two or three plus times.

[NSFW] Random Imgur

Shows you a random image from Imgur. Might occasionally show naked people or people of varying levels of aliveness, so tread carefully.

Anime Sedai

A list of most watched anime by year. Doubles as a checklist you can send to your friends, post on social media, or print and put in a frame next to your PhD you haven't put to good use.

Hunger Games Simulator

A relatively simple Hunger Games simulator for pitting your friends (or characters of choice) against one another and watching as said friends (or characters) either die in hilariously stupid or just plain gruesome ways.

A Website You Can Only Visit Once

Self-explanatory. A website where you can write advice for other people or read other people's advice. [bypass]

Visit a Random Website

An index of websites picked at random for your entertainment. Or at least that's what's advertised.

Hypertranslate

Takes text, sends it through Google Translate a couple of times and outputs the barely legible mess that was created.

Scrandle

Like Wordle, but you guess which of the two foods purchasable at a sports stadium people are more likely to eat, as per a community poll.

Calc-like

Little browser game I made in Godot for Mini Jam 183: Calculations. With potentially an improved version on the way. Yes, this is just a shameless plug. [In-site version]

Simon Tatham's Puzzles

Community reimplementations of bite-sized puzzles of surprising variety. Supports many platforms.

Pongchamp

A browser Pong recreation made in Godot by my friend Gosha. I swear I wasn't asked to put this here.

Hero of Law

An Ace Attorney crossover mod for The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time, created by Newer Team.
